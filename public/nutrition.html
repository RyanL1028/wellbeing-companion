Healthy Eating Tips

🥦 Eat the rainbow

Different colored fruits and vegetables provide different nutrients. Aim for variety!

💧 Stay hydrated

Drink water throughout the day. Thirst is often mistaken for hunger.

🍳 Don't skip breakfast

A balanced breakfast fuels your brain for morning classes and study sessions.

🥜 Smart snacking

Choose nuts, fruits, or yogurt over processed snacks for sustained energy.

🍽️ Mindful eating

Eat without screens when possible. Pay attention to hunger and fullness cues.

☕ Watch the caffeine

Limit caffeine after 2pm — it can disrupt sleep even hours later.
