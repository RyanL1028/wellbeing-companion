    Focus Tips

    🎯 The 2-minute rule

    If a task takes less than 2 minutes, do it now. Don't let small tasks pile up.

    📵 Phone away

    Put your phone in another room or use focus mode. Out of sight, out of mind.

    ⏰ Study in blocks

    Use the Pomodoro technique: 25 minutes of focus, then a 5-minute break.

    🎵 Background sounds

    Instrumental music or white noise can improve focus. Avoid lyrics while studying.

    📝 Active recall

    Test yourself instead of re-reading. Active recall is one of the most effective study methods.

    😴 Sleep matters

    Sleep is when your brain consolidates memories. Pulling an all-nighter hurts more than it helps.
